About

I was born and raised in Seattle, and graduated from Scripps College in Claremont, CA in 2014 with a BA in Politics and IR. After spending a year working at a law firm in Los Angeles and realizing that a legal career was not, in fact, for me, I started to dabble with coding and decided to take General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Course during Autumn 2015.

When I'm not writing code, you can find me discussing recent developments in American and international politics, geeking out over articles about driverless car tech, and taking pictures of my dog (Exhibit A to the right).
